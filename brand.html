I chose to create this logo because I’d like to create a clothing business that’s focused on creating or manufacturing sport outfits or wearable. Since I am a basketball player and aspire to be a professional player one day, why not create a business too that could help my career to grow? That’s why I chose this kind of business since if I’ll be a pro one day, my clothing would be popular because the owner of it is popular too.


The inspiration for this logo is from things that I saw inside our house especially the ball, the cog from my bike ( I like biking as well) and the blue banner from the color of our curtain. It is a simple yet cool looking design to be honest. Additionally, there’s two people who inspired me to take interest in clothing which is Cong Tv (Content Creator) with his “Payaman Shirt” and Sinio (FlipTopMC/Content Creator).
